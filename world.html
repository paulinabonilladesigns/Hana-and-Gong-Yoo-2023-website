About Haneul's World

Where can you find Haneul?

Haneul is from South Korea. In Hana's world however, South Korea has been experiencing an ongoing winter for over 75 years! Most trees are bare, and the ground is usually covered in snow. Some days are colder than others. It is necessary for you to bundle up if you are to spend time outside with Hana. When she's not at school, you'll find Haneul and Gong-Yoo traveling through Seoul on their adventures to bring back the seasons.

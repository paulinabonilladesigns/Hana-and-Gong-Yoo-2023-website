75 years ago, South Korea was struck by a sudden winter that has not gone away. Spring, Summer, and Fall have been dormant ever since...

This here is Haneul

Haneul is a five year old South Korean girl. You will often hear people refer to her as "Hana." Although she likes nature, she doesn’t get to spend much time outdoors because of the wintry weather. She is determined to change that this year. Hana and her loyal dog Gong-Yoo are on an adventure to restore the seasons.

Keep up with Haneul and Gong-Yoo on their adventures to end this 75-year-long winter and to finally see a a real life Jeju cherry blossom flower!
